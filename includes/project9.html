VVIP Flying

Department is responsible for ensuring safe and efficient of flying of VVIPs and Dignitaries including the Hon’ble Governor and the Chief Minister. The Department owns a Bell 429 Helicopter which was purchased in the 2012 at a cost of Rs. 36.72 Crores. The Department also hires flights / helicopters as and when required
