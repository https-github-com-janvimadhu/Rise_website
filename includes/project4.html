Development of Civil Enclave at Bhatinda
  • The Government of Punjab pursued the case of Airport at Bhatinda for increased connectivity to the underserved Malwa region.
  • Around 40 acres of land was acquired by the Government of Punjab at a cost of Rs. 3.55 crore at Village Virk Kalan, Bathinda for the construction of Civil Air Terminal and handed over to Airports Authority of India (AAI) free of cost.
  • The construction of the civil enclave building has been completed.
  • Air services under RCS-UDAN scheme have commenced from December 2016.
  • 3 flights are operating to Delhi and 7 flights per week for Jammu from this airport.
